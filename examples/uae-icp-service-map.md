# UAE ICP Service Map Example

Status: public-safe example  
Scope: source map, not final guidance  
Last reviewed: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

This example shows how official UAE government service pages can be converted into a founder-facing navigation map.

It is intentionally limited. It does not publish raw evidence, internal notes, source scoring, or private research records.

## Source Type

Official government service pages from the Federal Authority for Identity, Citizenship, Customs and Port Security (ICP).

## Public-Safe Service Map

| Founder Need | Public Service Area | Example Service |
| --- | --- | --- |
| First Emirates ID | Identity card services | New identity card issuance |
| Lost or damaged Emirates ID | Identity card services | Replacement for lost or damaged card |
| Update contact or address data | Identity card services | Data update |
| Renew Emirates ID | Identity card services | Identity card renewal |
| Visa pathway research | Visa services | Visa issuance service page |
| Residence permit research | Residence permit services | Residency permit issuance |
| Refund request | Refund and fine payment services | Refund of fees |

## What This Example Can Support

This example can support:

- founder navigation
- source discovery
- checklist planning
- identifying which official service area to inspect next

## What This Example Cannot Support

This example does not prove:

- eligibility
- required documents
- fee totals
- processing timelines
- application approval
- completeness of the full service catalogue

## Founder-Safe Use

Use this as a map, not as final advice.

Before publishing any service-specific checklist, verify the current official service page for:

- service title
- responsible government entity
- service code
- service steps
- required documents
- fees
- conditions
- channels
- completion duration
- last-updated date

## Editorial Rule

Do not turn a source map into a recommendation.

The safe public phrasing is:

> Start from the official ICP service page for the relevant identity, visa, or residence workflow, then verify the current requirements before acting.
