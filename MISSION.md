# Mission

Build a structured knowledge platform for founders.

The goal is to turn fragmented setup, banking, tax, visa, and operating knowledge into verified founder-facing intelligence that can be reused across products.

## Current Focus

- Dubai and UAE founder setup intelligence
- Public examples and templates after review
- Clear separation between private research operations and public outputs

## Operating Belief

Founders should not have to rebuild the same research from scattered browser tabs, old forum posts, sales pages, and half-remembered advice.

The platform exists to convert research into durable, source-backed, reusable outputs.

## Public Promise

Public material should be:

- clear
- source-aware
- cautious where facts are time-sensitive
- practical for founders
- explicit about what is known and what still needs verification

## First Public Theme

The first public theme is UAE founder infrastructure: setup, identity, residence, banking, tax, and operating workflows.
